DATATYPES
   
  NUMERIC TYPES:
int: Integers (e.g. -100).
float: Floating-point numbers (e.g.3.4).
complex: Complex numbers (e.g.1+2).

SEQUENCE TYPES:
str: (e.g.'Python').
list: square brackets (e.g. [1 "a"]).
tuple:  (e.g., (1, 2, "b")).
range: (e.g., range(5)).

MAPPING TYPES:
dict: curly braces (e.g., {"age": 30}).

SETTYPES:
set:  curly braces (e.g., {1, 2, 3}).
frozenset: Immutable version of a set.

BOOLEAN TYPES:
bool: True or False.
